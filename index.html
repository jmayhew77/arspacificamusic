Biography, vision, and ensemble members

Biography

Ars Pacifica Trio unites three acclaimed West Coast musicians — violin, cello, and piano — in vivid story‑forward programs. The ensemble’s name honors the Pacific’s breadth and the dialogue between classical heritage and contemporary voices. Recent highlights include festival invitations, residencies, and community workshops across Northern California.

Committed to education, Ars Pacifica leads masterclasses and interactive concerts that open the door to chamber music for new audiences, students, and veterans communities.
